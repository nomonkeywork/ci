# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Business teams and ops people who hate doing repetitive, mindless work themselves. Non-technical. Busy. They've tried tools that promised automation and got overwhelmed. They want someone else to handle the monkey work so they can focus on what matters.

## Product Purpose

nomonkeywork is an agency that takes repetitive, manual work off business teams' plates. Success looks like a visitor immediately understanding "these people will handle the tedious stuff I hate doing" and feeling confident enough to get in touch.

## Brand Personality

Playful, sharp, no-BS. The monkey mascot carries the wit; the copy and layout carry the confidence. Think: agency that's clearly competent but doesn't take itself too seriously.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: purple gradients, glassmorphism cards, floating feature grids
- Cluttered agency sites: too many scroll animations, unclear hierarchy, everything shouting
- Corporate/enterprise tone: stiff, jargon-heavy, soul-free

## Design Principles

1. **Let the mascot do the talking.** The monkey is the brand's personality anchor. Everything around it should give it room to breathe and land.
2. **Sharp clarity over decoration.** One message per section. No filler copy. No decorative elements that don't earn their place.
3. **Confidence without volume.** The page doesn't need to shout. Strong typography hierarchy and intentional whitespace say "we know what we're doing" louder than gradients do.
4. **Playful but professional.** Wit in the details (mascot, naming), seriousness in the structure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Mascot interactions (eye tracking) should degrade gracefully with reduced-motion preferences.

## Slogan

description: "We do the monkey work. You do the real work."
